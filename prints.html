Illustrative City Prints

Combining my love of travel and designing, I remake maps and print illustrations for the cities I have traveled to. What started out as a fun project has now turned into a hobby after I travel to a new city. This has barely scratched the surface for the places I have been / want to go to, but it is a fun way to play around with different mediums / styles of illustrations. This is for fun and not for a specific client or project, I am simply fascinated by maps and city illustrations.
My plan is to create a 'city guide' for the places I know the best and illustrate the best points of interest, restaurants, attractions, etc.
