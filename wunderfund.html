Wunderfund

To put it simply, Wunderfund is an equity crowdfunding platform to help startups raise up to $1M from everyday people. While working as an ad agency closely with startups, we realized the biggest struggle they have: raising capital. We built this platform over the last 2 years, got certified by FINRA and the SEC in October, and now have 3 successful campaigns and constantly putting up new ones. I was the lead designer on this developing everything you see on the front-end to managing the marketing, educational pieces, and building the campaigns you see for certain startups. Although there are screenshots on this page, you should really check out the real thing.
