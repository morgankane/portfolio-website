Wunderfund 2.0

To put it simply, Wunderfund is an equity crowdfunding platform to help startups raise up to $1M from everyday people. We developed and launched the MVP in October of 2016 and ever since, have started on what we're calling '2.0'. We learned a lot with the MVP about our users, SEC and FINRA compliance, what our competitors are doing and now we are working to put out a version that's developed and designed on a higher scale. Right now, since it's just in development phase, I only have screenshots of the design and our style guide I designed.
