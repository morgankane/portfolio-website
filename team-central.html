Team Central

Team Central was a client we took on as a marketing agency to build a simple website, but they then brought us on to do more work, including the design of their platform. Team Central is a software that analyzes and learns your business to then tell you exactly what you need to know for that day - from proposals, on boarding employees, staffing, to sending you documents you'll need for that meeting you have later. The technology was there, I just assisted with website and platform design to build a stronger brand.
